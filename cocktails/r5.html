Daiquiri

The Daiquiri is a refreshingly simple yet incredibly satisfying cocktail, hailing from Cuba.
Its perfect blend of rum, lime, and sugar creates a harmonious balance of sweet and tart.
It is a lovely choice for warm weather or any occasion where a bright, zesty drink is desired.
Its elegance lies in its simplicity, showcasing the quality of its core ingredients.
