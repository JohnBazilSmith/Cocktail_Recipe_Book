White Russian

The White Russian is a creamy and sweet cocktail with a rich, velvety texture and coffee-chocolate notes.
It's a comforting and satisfying choice, especially after a meal or as a nightcap.
The cocktail gained significant popularity thanks to the film "The Big Lebowski."
It's essentially a variation of the Black Russian, with the addition of cream or milk,
transforming it into a smooth, dessert-like drink.
