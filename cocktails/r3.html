Espresso Martini

The Espresso Martini is a sophisticated and energizing cocktail.
It blends the robust flavor of coffee with the crispness of vodka and the sweetness of a coffee liqueur.
It's a relatively modern classic, believed to have been created in the 1980s by bartender Dick Bradsell
in London for a model who requested a drink that would "wake me up and then f*** me up."
Its frothy top and rich aroma make it an appealing after-dinner drink or a pick-me-up.
